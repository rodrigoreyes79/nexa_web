## Who it's for

NexaUno is built for two kinds of teams:

- **SMBs running online payments** — online stores and service businesses that take payments through Stripe or Mollie and lose hours every month reconciling payouts against orders.
- **Fintech consulting agencies** — consultancies that implement and operate payments for their clients and need a reliable, repeatable reconciliation layer they can deploy across multiple merchants.

## Why reconciliation matters

When money moves through a payment processor, what lands in your bank account is never a simple sum of your sales. Processor fees, refunds, chargebacks, currency conversion and settlement timing all change the numbers. Reconciliation is the process of proving that every euro you earned is accounted for — and finding the ones that aren't.

NexaUno automates that proof, continuously, so your team can trust the books instead of rebuilding them in a spreadsheet.
