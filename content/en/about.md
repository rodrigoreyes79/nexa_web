## Our mission

NexaUno builds software that removes the operational friction from electronic payments. Accepting a payment online is easy; *reconciling* it — proving that every transaction, fee and payout is accounted for — is still painful, manual and error-prone for most businesses. We're here to fix that.

## What we're building

Our first product automates **payment reconciliation** between modern payment processors (Stripe and Mollie) and the e-commerce platforms businesses actually run on (Magento and WooCommerce). From there, we're expanding into broader integration and reconciliation tooling for electronic payments.

## Who we serve

- **Small and medium businesses** that take payments online and want their books to tie out without the spreadsheet marathon.
- **Fintech consulting agencies** that need a dependable reconciliation layer to deploy across the merchants they serve.

## Who's behind it

NexaUno is an independent business based in the Community of Madrid, Spain, operated by Rodrigo Reyes as a registered self-employed professional (*autónomo*). Being small and focused means we work closely with our early customers and ship improvements quickly.

We operate under Spanish and EU law, including the GDPR, and we take the security of payment-related data seriously: we use official processor APIs, request only the access we need, and never store more than required to do the job.
