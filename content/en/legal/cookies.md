## What are cookies?

Cookies are small files that a website stores on your device to make it work or to provide information to the site's owners. Similar technologies (such as local storage) may be used for the same purposes.

## Cookies used on this website

This is a static informational website that aims to use as few cookies as possible.

- **Strictly necessary / technical cookies** — used only where required to deliver the site securely (for example, by our hosting or content delivery provider). These do not require consent under Spanish and EU law because they are essential for the website to function.
- **Fonts** — this site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. Depending on configuration, this may involve a request to Google's servers. If you prefer to avoid this, fonts can be self-hosted; contact us if this is a concern.

We do **not** currently use advertising, profiling or third-party analytics cookies. If we introduce analytics or other non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and request your consent through a cookie banner before setting them.

## Managing cookies

You can allow, block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Each browser is different; consult your browser's help pages for instructions. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect how the website works.

## Updates

We may update this Cookies Policy to reflect changes in the technologies we use or in applicable law. The date of the latest revision is shown at the top of this page.
