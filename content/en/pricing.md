## Frequently asked questions

### Is there a free trial?
We're onboarding early customers individually right now. [Get in touch](/contact/) and we'll set you up with a trial that fits your volume.

### How am I billed?
Plans are billed monthly in euros (EUR), excluding VAT. As a Spanish *autónomo*, NexaUno issues compliant invoices for every payment.

### Can I cancel?
Yes — subscriptions can be cancelled at any time and will not renew for the following period. See our [refund and cancellation policy](/legal/refunds/) for details.
